03 // STRATEGIC FRAMEWORK

Understanding the Medium

Every platform operates by different rules, and the medium fundamentally shapes how audiences process information. What works on X fails on Instagram. Cable news demands authoritative presentation and production polish; social platforms reward emotional immediacy and conversational authenticity.

Effective communication requires fluency across these mediums—not just adapting content, but reconceptualizing it entirely for each distribution environment.

Infrastructure & Velocity

Translating medium theory into operational practice requires technical infrastructure and production velocity. Over a decade across congressional committees, cable news, and national media, I've built workflows that operationalize these principles: rapid-response systems producing 30+ platform-optimized assets daily, and analytics dashboards identifying which emotional framings drive engagement in real time.
